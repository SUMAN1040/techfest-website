About Techfest

Techfest IIT Bombay, established in 1998, has grown to become Asia’s largest science and technology festival. It is a platform where innovation meets inspiration, drawing over 175,000 visitors annually and reaching millions worldwide through its diverse initiatives.

Driven by a vision to promote technology, scientific thinking, and innovation, Techfest bridges the gap between industry leaders, budding engineers, researchers, and students. Each edition of Techfest is a grand confluence of exhibitions, competitions, workshops, talks, and performances.

Highlights:

  • Over 75+ international and national events and competitions
  • Renowned speakers from top tech and research fields
  • Interactive exhibitions showcasing cutting-edge technology
  • Workshops with hands-on training in emerging technologies
  • Initiatives focusing on social causes and sustainability

From cutting-edge robotics to artificial intelligence challenges, Techfest continues to be a beacon of technological advancement and a place where ideas turn into reality. Join us and be part of the movement that fuels Asia’s biggest tech revolution.
